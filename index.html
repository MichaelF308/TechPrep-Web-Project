Avalon Adventures provides excellent recreational activites for individuals and families alike.

Whale Watching

Join us for a 'whale of a good time' with whale watching and the traditional Newfoundland pastime of cod jigging!

Family Ferryland Adventure

For the family! Sail across the wonderous sea to the historical Colony of Avalon and a picnic near the Lighthouse!

Family Culture Package

An exciting family package for visitors to St. John's that includes visits to The Rooms and the Johnson Geo Centre!

Adult Brewery Crawl

Experience the thriving craft beer scene on the Avalon Peninsula with our exclusive group adult package!
